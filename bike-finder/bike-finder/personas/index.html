Needs Transportation

Sam is in his mid-20s and a recent grad. He lives in Portland. He has been working in a Marketing Department for 2 years and he earns 50K/year. It takes him 30 minutes to walk to work but only 8 minutes to bike. Although, the hilly terrain makes the commute somewhat difficult. His bike got stolen awhile ago and he been saving money for a new bike. He needs a bike as soon as possible because winter is coming soon. Sam also lives on the 4th floor of a small apartment with one other roommate. Unfortunately, there's no elevator in the apartment.

Sam is in average shape but doesn't work out. He values efficiency and is frugal. While he wants a bike now, he sees himself buying a car in the upcoming years. He has intermediate knowledge of bikes. He can differentiate different types of bikes, knows how to shift gears, and understands basic concept of bike. As a millennial, Sam is good at using technology.
